Comprehensive Social Media Marketing Campaign: PYUR Skincare

Project Overview

In this project, I developed a comprehensive social media marketing campaign for PYUR, a British multinational skincare brand. The campaign aimed to increase awareness of PYUR's new 3-step skincare solution in key U.S. markets, targeting men and women aged 21-45 who lead active lifestyles and care about healthy skin.

Organic Social Strategy

Insights and Recommendations

Based on the analysis of the Facebook Organic Data, I identified the following insights and recommendations:

  1. Insight: Video content significantly outperformed photo content in terms of reach, impressions, and engagement.
    Recommendation: Increase the production of video content across all content themes to boost overall engagement and reach.
  2. Insight: Testimonial-themed content consistently performed well, especially in video format.
    Recommendation: Focus on creating more testimonial videos to leverage this high-performing content type.
  3. Insight: Posts published on Saturdays, particularly around 8:00 AM and 4:00 PM, showed higher engagement.
    Recommendation: Optimize the posting schedule to include more content on Saturdays, especially during these peak hours.

Platform Selection

Based on the campaign objectives and target audience, I've selected the following platforms:

  1. Facebook: Ideal for sharing testimonials and building community engagement around PYUR's products.
  2. Instagram: Perfect for showcasing visually appealing product images and lifestyle content that resonates with our target audience.
  3. TikTok: Excellent for creating short, engaging video content that can reach our younger demographic and showcase quick skincare routines.

Growth Strategy

To grow our social media audience, we will:

  • Implement a user-generated content campaign encouraging customers to share their PYUR skincare routines
  • Collaborate with micro-influencers in the beauty and lifestyle niches
  • Host weekly live Q&A sessions on Instagram to engage with our audience directly
  • Create a branded hashtag challenge on TikTok to increase brand awareness and engagement

Paid Social Strategy

Insights and Recommendations

Based on the analysis of the Facebook Paid Data, I identified the following insights and recommendations:

  1. Insight: Video ads (Awareness Testimonial) achieved a significantly lower CPM compared to static image ads.
    Recommendation: Allocate more budget to video-based ads, particularly testimonials, to maximize reach and impressions.
  2. Insight: The static image campaign (Product Feature) generated higher post engagement despite lower reach and impressions.
    Recommendation: Use static image ads for specific engagement-focused campaigns, but prioritize video for broader awareness goals.
  3. Insight: The Testimonial campaign resulted in more than double the number of clicks compared to the Product Feature campaign.
    Recommendation: Focus on creating compelling testimonial content for future campaigns to drive higher click-through rates.

Campaign Details

  • Campaign Objective: Increase awareness of PYUR's new 3-step skincare solution in key U.S. markets
  • Budget: $8,000 for paid media, $2,000 for influencer campaign
  • Platforms: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok

Audience Targeting

  • Demographics: Men and women, aged 21-45
  • Geo-targeting: New York, Chicago, Miami, Dallas, Houston, and Los Angeles
  • Behavioral targeting: Interest in skincare, health and wellness, active lifestyles

Ad for Men

A/B Testing Plan

Goal: Determine which ad copy is more effective in driving engagement and clicks for men vs. women. After the copy has been tested, we can test for other variables, such as imagery.

Next steps: After running the test, analyze the performance metrics (CTR, engagement rate, conversion rate) for each ad variant. Use the insights to refine future ad creatives and targeting strategies for each gender segment.
